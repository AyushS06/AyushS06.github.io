What 4 Years offers to students

As a College Consulting Service, this company was co-founded by 2 students, Ayush Sharma and Manoj Niverthi, who both have experienced the long and weary path to college!


This company is designed to help international and domestic students gain admittance into U.S. universities!

Services

The Accepted Student Database (ASD) is a unique product from 4 Years where users can ask advice from students who have been accepted to their dream university! For example, if you searched in our database under the search category - College Acceptances, "Georgia Tech", you will get a list of students who were accepted by Georgia Tech! You can contact them either through Email or Instgram messaging, and can ask them questions about the university!
We provide vital information about U.S. universities such as average cost of attendance, standardized test scores, earnings post graduation, as well as the retention and completion rates for all students!
There are thousands upon thousands tutoring centers and corporations located around the United States. What we plan to do first is to connect with these tutoring centers and corporations in our local state, Georgia.
